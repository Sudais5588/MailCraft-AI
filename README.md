## AI Email Copywriter Pipeline

MailCraft AI is an AI Agent Engineering project built for the SiteArche Virtual Hackathon. It helps students, freelancers, startups, and small businesses generate professional sales email sequences from simple input.

The tool takes product details, target audience, campaign goal, tone, key benefit, and call-to-action, then generates a structured email campaign with quality analysis.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Writing professional sales emails can be difficult, especially for students, freelancers, and small businesses. Many people struggle with writing clear subject lines, strong email bodies, and effective call-to-actions.

MailCraft AI solves this problem by generating a complete sales email sequence and evaluating each email using built-in quality checks.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many users do not know how to write high-quality sales emails that are clear, persuasive, and professional.

Common problems include:

- Weak subject lines
- Poor email structure
- Missing call-to-action
- Spam-like wording
- Lack of follow-up sequence

---

## 💡 Solution

MailCraft AI generates a structured email campaign based on user input.

The app creates:

- Awareness email
- Value email
- Trust email
- Offer email
- Final reminder email

It also checks each email and provides:

- Email quality score
- Spam-word detection
- Subject line feedback
- Call-to-action check
- Overall campaign score

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Python
- Streamlit
- Rule-based AI logic
- Email quality analysis system

---

## ✨ Features

- Generate professional sales email sequences
- Select tone: Professional, Friendly, Persuasive, or Urgent
- Create 1 to 5 emails
- Campaign strategy generation
- Email quality scoring
- Spam-word checking
- Editable email output
- Download generated campaign as a text file

---

## 🔄 How It Works

1. User enters product or service details
2. User selects target audience and email goal
3. User chooses email tone and number of emails
4. System generates a structured campaign strategy
5. System creates email subject lines and email bodies
6. Each email is analyzed using quality checks
7. User can download the final email sequence
